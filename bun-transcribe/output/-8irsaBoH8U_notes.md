# Cornerstone Chapel Annual Post-Thanksgiving Review

## Speakers
- Pastor Gary (introduction)
- Marwan Rifka (elder update, passing torch after 8 years)
- Pastor Andy Wagner (main annual update)

## Overview
- 8th annual review of God's faithfulness and work at the church.
- Inspired by late member David's call to share church activities for collective thanksgiving.
- Emphasis: God's deeds (Exodus 15:11), not self-praise; methods change, mission (making Him known) constant.

## Volunteers & Service
- 350 volunteers each Sunday; 3,000 annual opportunities filled.
- Roles: children's ministry, parking, greeters, ushers, security, nursery, worship, tech, outreach.
- Reputation as serving community affirmed by Leesburg Police captain.

## U.S. Outreach
- Harvest Party: 150 volunteers/staff, 6,000 attendees (alternative to Halloween).
- 1,000 volunteer hours; 17 local projects, 3 distributions (food/supplies).
- Helped refugee family from China (apartment, school, integration).
- 5 trips to Asheville, NC post-Hurricane Helene.
- Operation Christmas Child: 6,500 shoeboxes (4,500 local, 1,200 online, 600 from CCA students).

## Global Outreach
- 6 trips to 4 countries (e.g., Uganda: villages, water, gospel, medicine).
- Christmas Catalog (last year: $275k from 856 donations):

  - $39k: education/vocational (e.g., anesthesia machine Uganda).

  - Vehicles for Chile/Hungary missionaries.
  - 115 blanket/pillow sets for Malawi orphanage.
