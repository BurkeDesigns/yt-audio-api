# Stand Your Ground Against the Enemy

## Scripture Focus
- Ephesians 6:10-18 (armor of God, spiritual warfare)
- Ephesians 6:19-24 (Paul's closing)

## Ephesians Postures
- Chapters 1-3: Seated in Christ (Eph 1:20; Eph 2:6)
- Chapters 4-5: Walk worthy (Eph 4:1; walk in love, light, wisdom)
- Chapter 6: Stand firm (Eph 6:10-14)

## Spiritual Warfare Reality
- Unseen but real battle against Satan/demons (not flesh/blood; Eph 6:12)
- 56% of Americans believe Satan is real (Barna study)
- Targets believers to destroy marriages, families, faith, churches
- Satan: roaring lion (1 Pet 5:8), tempter (1 Thess 3:5), dragon/serpent/deceiver (Rev 12:9), murderer/liar (John 8:44)
- Origin: Satan's rebellion, 1/3 angels cast out (Rev 12:7-9; Isa 14:12; Luke 10:18); now demons on earth
- Angels minister to believers (Heb 1:14)
- Believers: oppressed (not possessed) from outside (1 John 4:4; Luke 13 spirit of infirmity)

## Key Instructions (Eph 6)
- Know real enemy: Satan/demons (principalities, powers, rulers of darkness; Eph 6:12)
- Stand firm, no retreat ("stand" x3; Eph 6:11,13,14; James 4:7)
- Strength in Lord's power (Eph 6:10; Jude 9)
- Wear full armor of God (Eph 6:14-18)

## Armor of God
### Defensive
- Belt of truth: Know God's Word to discern lies
- Breastplate of righteousness: Guard heart (Prov 4:23)
- Feet with gospel of peace: Share gospel, advance kingdom
- Shield of faith: Quench fiery darts (fear; 2 Tim 1:7)
- Helmet of salvation: Protect mind, take thoughts captive (2 Cor 10:5)

### Offensive
- Sword of Spirit (Word of God): Quote Scripture (Luke 4)
- Prayer/supplication: Persistent intercession (Eph 6:18)

## Closing
- Invitation for battle-weary to stand for prayer
- Prayer for strength, deliverance (Isa 59:19)
